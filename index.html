What we do

NeverForget is a cloud-based reminder application. Users can create and manage personal reminders and receive notifications by email and push. We offer a Free plan (limited reminders, email notifications) and a Premium subscription (unlimited reminders, additional channels, advanced scheduling).

Service type: Software as a Service (SaaS), delivered instantly online after signup. No physical goods are sold.
